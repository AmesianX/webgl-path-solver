WebGL Shortest Path Solver

This maze is being solved entirely on your GPU as a 12-state cellular automata. All branches are searched in parallel (green) as they are discovered, leaving a trail of breadcrumbs pointing to the start (red). As soon as the end cell (red) is reached, a route is traced (blue) along the breadcrumb root back to the start. This final route trace would be better done on the CPU.
